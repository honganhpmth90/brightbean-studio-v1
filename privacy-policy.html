Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 09, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Post manager collects, uses, and protects information when users access and use our Fanpage management application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the application, we may access certain information necessary to provide Fanpage management features, including:

  • Basic Facebook account information such as your name, user ID, or email address if permission is granted.
  • Information related to the Facebook Pages you manage, including page name, page ID, and related access permissions.
  • Content or data required to support features such as post management, messaging, comments, or Fanpage analytics, depending on the permissions you authorize.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information you authorize for the following purposes:

  • To provide Fanpage management functionality to users.
  • To display, process, or manage Fanpage data based on user requests.
  • To improve service quality and user experience.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and protect the integrity of the service.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared in the following cases:

  • With the user’s consent.
  • When necessary to operate the service requested by the user.
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights and the safety of users.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data.
  • Revoke the application’s permissions through Facebook account settings.
  • Stop using the application at any time.

6. User Data Deletion

Users may revoke the application's permissions at any time through their Facebook account settings. Any stored data associated with the application may be removed in accordance with applicable policies and regulations.

7. Facebook/Meta Compliance

This application may use Facebook/Meta APIs and services. The use of Facebook data complies with Meta Platform Policies and related terms.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time when necessary. The updated version will be published on this page and becomes effective from the updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the application administrator directly.
